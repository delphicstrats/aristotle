## Why They're a Candidate

S&P Global operates a sophisticated multi-geography government affairs function with documented Quorum implementation. Their team tracks legislative and regulatory developments across the U.S., Europe, and Asia, with structured meeting reporting to executives. They've moved beyond Excel and built an ROI measurement discipline for public affairs — precisely the mindset that makes SCI's cross-domain value proposition legible. As a financial data company, they also face multi-domain pressures: regulatory (Government), market intelligence (Market), ESG narrative (Information), and systemic risk positioning (all four domains).
