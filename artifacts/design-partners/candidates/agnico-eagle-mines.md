## Why They're a Candidate

Agnico Eagle Mines is a documented Borealis customer using the platform specifically to demonstrate social performance and manage their "social license to operate" — the Civil Society domain made explicit. As a major international gold mining company operating in Canada, Mexico, Finland, and elsewhere, they manage simultaneous stakeholder pressure from Indigenous communities, environmental NGOs, regulators, host governments, and capital markets. This is textbook multi-domain governance. Their Borealis investment signals they've accepted that community engagement is a formal governance function, not an ad-hoc exercise.
