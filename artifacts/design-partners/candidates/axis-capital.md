## Why They're a Candidate

Joe Cohen, Chief Marketing and Communications Officer at AXIS Capital, was a featured speaker at the 2025 Page Annual Conference — appearing on the polarization panel and at the Annual General Meeting. AXIS is a specialty insurance and reinsurance company with significant exposure to climate risk, which creates multi-domain stakeholder complexity: government (insurance regulation across multiple jurisdictions, climate policy), information (narrative about insurance market contraction, climate risk pricing), civil society (community impact of insurance availability/affordability), and market (institutional investors evaluating climate exposure). AXIS is appropriately sized — large enough to have genuine complexity, small enough that a CCO can actually move on a pilot decision.

## SCI Fit

AXIS's SCI fit centers on the Market + Government + Information domain intersection: specialty insurance companies must simultaneously navigate regulatory environments (state insurance commissioners, international regulators), investor relations around catastrophe risk, and media narrative about insurance availability — particularly acute given climate-driven insurance market contraction in high-risk geographies.

## Notes

The specialty insurance/reinsurance sector is undergoing significant disruption from climate risk — insurers are withdrawing from Florida, California, and Louisiana, creating government relations crises simultaneously with media narrative crises. AXIS's position as a global specialty insurer gives them a unique vantage point on multi-domain governance under continuous external pressure. Cohen's dual role (Marketing AND Communications) under one function may create a more integrated starting point for SCI adoption.
