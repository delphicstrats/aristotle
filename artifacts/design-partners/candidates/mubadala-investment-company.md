## Why They're a Candidate

Brian Lott, Chief Communications Officer at Mubadala, was a featured speaker at the 2025 Page Annual Conference — speaking specifically at the Annual General Meeting of the Membership, a role reserved for the most senior and trusted Page members. Mubadala is Abu Dhabi's sovereign wealth fund with investments across aerospace, healthcare, technology, energy, and real estate globally. As a state-owned investment vehicle operating in Western markets, Mubadala simultaneously manages government stakeholder relations (multiple governments — UAE, US, EU, and investee-country governments), market stakeholders (co-investors, portfolio company boards, capital markets), civil society (ESG scrutiny, geopolitical optics), and information (narrative in both Arabic and English-language media ecosystems). This is four-domain governance at sovereign scale.
