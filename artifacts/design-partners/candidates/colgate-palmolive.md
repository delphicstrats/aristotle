## Why They're a Candidate

Dana Bolden, CCO at Colgate-Palmolive, was specifically named and quoted in Signal AI's "Top Corporate Reputation Challenges in 2025" report — indicating C-suite level adoption of an AI-driven, multi-signal intelligence platform. Colgate-Palmolive faces all four SCI domains: government (regulatory, labeling, trade policy across 200+ markets), information (media, social, brand narrative), civil society (sustainability, ESG, supply chain scrutiny), and market (investor relations, activist campaigns, ESG ratings). Their CCO is already using Signal AI to monitor reputation multi-dimensionally; SCI would give that monitoring governance structure and cross-domain integration.
