## Why They're a Candidate

Lenovo is a documented RepTrak client that used reputation data to drive a global communications strategy — aligning their messaging architecture against RepTrak's reputation driver model across governance, citizenship, and innovation pillars. Charlotte West, VP of Global Corporate Communications, was specifically featured in RepTrak's LinkedIn communications about the case study. This signals board-level reputation governance investment. Lenovo also faces extreme multi-domain complexity: operating between China and the West creates simultaneous government (trade policy, export controls), information (narrative in competing media ecosystems), civil society (sustainability, labor), and market (institutional investor) pressures.

## SCI Fit

Lenovo's SCI fit is strongest in the Information + Government domain intersection: managing corporate narrative simultaneously across Chinese and Western media ecosystems, under ongoing geopolitical pressure, while trying to maintain a globally coherent reputation. The design partner conversation would focus on how multi-market stakeholder governance differs from single-market approaches, and how SCI could help coordinate government, media, and investor stakeholder strategies.
