## Key Findings for Platform Team

1. **Quorum → SCI is a natural upgrade path for government affairs teams** who want to extend their stakeholder intelligence beyond the Government domain. S&P Global and DTE Energy are the clearest examples.

2. **Borealis → SCI is the integration layer** that connects Borealis's Civil Society governance data to Government, Market, and Information domains. Agnico Eagle and Trans Mountain are ready for this conversation.

3. **Signal AI → SCI adds governance structure** to the media/reputation intelligence Signal AI users are already consuming. KPMG and Colgate-Palmolive are already using Signal AI at CCO level and are ready for the next step.

4. **RepTrak → SCI is the action layer** that transforms RepTrak's reputation measurement data into stakeholder governance decisions. Lenovo is confirmed; the broader RepTrak Global 100 client base is a large secondary target list.

5. **Page Society → the CCO community is the right entry point**, not the government affairs or IR function. Travis Parman (PMI), Whitney Eichinger (Southwest), and Brian Lott (Mubadala) are the highest-value Page Society candidates.
